Our modern japanese concept extends to zakabaran

the cafe/ restaurant/bar on WIRED HOTEL's lobby floor

Come by for a meal, a cup of coffee in the afternoon , or a drink after day of exploring

Enjoy traditional Japanese cuisine with a modern twist with our ever changing seasonal menu.

Everything is designed to pair perfectly with our sake selection

Though Japanese sake - or nihonshu - has gained in

popularity overseas, there is still many sakes that never

leave Japan. We have a wide selection of unique sakes to

match any meal or mood.

Eschewing the stale breakfast buffet of other hotels, we offer individual plates of traditional

Japanese breakfast. We also offer tofu donuts or other light Western style meals.

We have partnered with local Japanese sweets makers and arranged them for

our unique and original menu items.

Zakabaran is also our main community hub where you can hang out with locals, or come by for one of our many community

events.
